About

I am a PhD student in the theory group at UCSD, where I am advised by Shachar Lovett. Before that, I worked at Microsoft for three years. I received a BS and MS in Mathematics and Computing from Indian Institute of Technology, Delhi (IITD) advised by Subiman Kundu.

I am currently pursuing research in learning theory and reinforcement learning.

In Fall 2019, I was a short term scholar at Institute for Advanced Study. In Summer 2019, I was a visiting graduate student at Simons Institute.
